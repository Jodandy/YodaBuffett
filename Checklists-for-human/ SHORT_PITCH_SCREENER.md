SHORT PITCH SCREENER v1.0
Identifying 30%+ Downside Opportunities
═══════════════════════════════════════════

CORE PHILOSOPHY
"Invert, always invert." - Charlie Munger
Goal: Identify companies with 30%+ downside potential over 6-18 months where:

Valuation doesn't reflect deteriorating reality
Financial quality is declining (often masked)
Story is cracking but market hasn't noticed yet
Early warning signs are visible before the obvious break

This is NOT about finding obvious frauds. Those are rare.
This IS about finding:

Overvalued companies meeting reality
Deteriorating fundamentals covered up by accounting
Business models under structural pressure
Peak cycle stories priced for perfection


WHEN TO USE THIS SCREENER
Use the Short Pitch Screener when you suspect:

Company is significantly overvalued
Financial quality deteriorating but hidden
Business model under pressure
Story starting to crack
Management credibility declining
Insiders selling heavily

Common triggers:

Stock trading at extreme valuations (>10x revenue, >40x EBIT)
Company "beats" every quarter but quality declining
Margins compressing but explained away
Working capital quietly deteriorating
Aggressive acquisition strategy masking organic decline
Too many "one-time" adjustments


SYSTEM OVERVIEW
Three-Tier Process:
TIER 1: AI SHORT ANALYSIS (15 minutes)

AI analyzes company for short potential
Identifies what makes it a potential 30%+ decline
Generates verification tasks for bearish thesis
Output: Short Analysis + QA Instructions

TIER 2: HUMAN QA VERIFICATION (30-40 minutes)

Verify AI's bearish claims
Check for hidden strengths (could invalidate short)
Look for borrow availability and costs
Assess squeeze risk
Output: SHORT / PASS / NEEDS MORE RESEARCH

TIER 3: SHORT DECISION (15 minutes)

Assess risk/reward (remember: unlimited upside risk!)
Calculate conservative position size (2-5% max)
Set tight stop losses
Define cover/profit-taking strategy
Output: SHORT [size] / PASS / WATCHLIST

Total time: 60-70 minutes per short candidate

CRITICAL SHORT MISTAKES TO AVOID
The 10 Deadly Short Mistakes:
1. Shorting Just Because "It's Expensive"
❌ Mistake: "P/E is 100x, it has to go down!"
✅ Reality: Expensive can get more expensive. Need catalyst for decline.
Fix: Only short if overvalued AND deteriorating fundamentals AND catalyst.

2. Ignoring Squeeze Risk
❌ Mistake: "I'll just ride through the squeeze, I'm right long-term."
✅ Reality: You can be right and still lose everything in squeeze.
Fix: Always check short interest. If >15%, reduce size or avoid.

3. Position Sizing Like Longs
❌ Mistake: "This is 10% conviction, so 10% position."
✅ Reality: Losses are unlimited. Never size shorts like longs.
Fix: Maximum 5% per short, 15% total shorts, regardless of conviction.

4. No Stop Loss
❌ Mistake: "I'll wait for thesis to play out, no matter the price."
✅ Reality: Stock can double while you wait. Destroys portfolio.
Fix: Always have stop loss. Typically 25-30%. Use it.

5. Fighting Momentum
❌ Mistake: "It's up 50% in a month, perfect time to short the top!"
✅ Reality: Momentum can continue longer than you can stay solvent.
Fix: Wait for topping formation, momentum shift, or negative catalyst.

6. Shorting Cult Stocks
❌ Mistake: "Fundamentals are terrible, easy short."
✅ Reality: Cult following doesn't care about fundamentals. Will squeeze you.
Fix: Avoid stocks with strong retail following, meme status, or charismatic CEOs.

7. Averaging Down
❌ Mistake: "It went up, now it's even MORE overvalued, I'll add to short."
✅ Reality: Market can stay irrational longer than you can stay solvent.
Fix: Rarely add to shorts. If up >20%, reassess thesis rather than adding.

8. Ignoring Carrying Costs
❌ Mistake: "Borrow is 12%, no big deal."
✅ Reality: 12% borrow + 3% dividend = 15% annual drag. Kills returns.
Fix: Calculate total carry. If >10% annually, need quick thesis or pass.

9. Timing Too Early
❌ Mistake: "Problems are obvious, I'll short now before everyone else sees."
✅ Reality: Market can ignore problems for years. You pay carry waiting.
Fix: Wait for catalyst or early signs market noticing. Don't short too early.

10. Not Covering on Thesis Break
❌ Mistake: "It's up 40% but thesis hasn't changed, I'll hold."
✅ Reality: If thesis isn't working, something is wrong. Cut loss.
Fix: If short up >30% and no progress after 6mo, cover and reassess.
