# AAK Fat Pitch Evaluation Framework

## Company Overview
AAK (AarhusKarlshamn) is a Swedish specialty oils and fats company serving the food, chocolate & confectionery, and technical industries.

## Fat Pitch Checklist Evaluation

### 1. Opportunity Assessment

#### Upside Potential:
- [ ] **50-100%+ upside over 1-3 years?**
  - Need to assess: Current stock price vs historical valuations
  - Key drivers: Specialty fats market growth, margin expansion potential
  - Market size: Global specialty fats market growing at ~5-7% CAGR

#### Risk/Reward Ratio:
- [ ] **Limited downside relative to upside?**
  - Consider: Raw material price volatility (palm oil, shea, etc.)
  - Business model: B2B with long-term contracts provides stability
  - Geographic diversification across Europe, Americas, Asia

### 2. Business Quality Assessment

#### Competitive Position:
- [ ] **Sustainable competitive advantage?**
  - Moat factors: Technical expertise in specialty fats, customer relationships
  - R&D capabilities and co-development with customers
  - Market position: One of the leading specialty fats companies globally

#### Market Position:
- [ ] **Market share trends?**
  - Focus on higher-margin specialty solutions vs commodity oils
  - Plant-based food trend beneficiary
  - Chocolate & confectionery segment strength

### 3. Timing and Technical Considerations

#### Market Context:
- [ ] **Sector/market conditions?**
  - Food ingredients sector defensive characteristics
  - ESG trends favoring sustainable sourcing (AAK focus area)
  - Input cost inflation concerns

### 4. Conviction Test

#### Key Questions to Answer from Q2 2025 Report:
1. **Revenue Growth**: Is organic growth accelerating? Target: >5-7%
2. **Margin Expansion**: Are EBIT margins improving? Target: >8-9%
3. **Volume vs Price**: Is growth driven by volume or just pricing?
4. **Segment Performance**: Which segments driving growth?
5. **Geographic Mix**: Performance by region?
6. **Cash Flow**: Operating cash flow trends?
7. **Guidance**: Management outlook and confidence?

### 5. Position Sizing Considerations

#### Starting Point:
- Large-cap Swedish company (likely $2-5B market cap)
- Food ingredients = defensive but cyclical margins
- Suggested initial size: 5-7% if thesis strong

#### Adjustments:
- Volatility: Medium (commodity input exposure)
- Liquidity: Good (listed on Nasdaq Stockholm)
- Sector concentration: Check existing consumer staples exposure

### Key Metrics to Monitor

#### Financial Health Indicators:
- [ ] Operating margin trends (target: consistent >8%)
- [ ] ROIC (target: >12-15%)
- [ ] Net debt/EBITDA (target: <2.5x)
- [ ] Organic volume growth (target: 3-5%)

#### Business Health Indicators:
- [ ] Innovation pipeline (new product launches)
- [ ] Customer concentration risk
- [ ] Raw material sourcing sustainability
- [ ] Market share in key segments

### Investment Thesis Summary

**Bull Case:**
- Structural growth in plant-based foods
- Premiumization trend in food ingredients
- Technical expertise creating switching costs
- ESG leadership in sustainable sourcing
- Margin expansion from specialty focus

**Bear Case:**
- Raw material cost volatility
- Customer concentration risks
- Competition from larger players
- Economic slowdown impacting volumes
- Currency exposure (SEK based)
